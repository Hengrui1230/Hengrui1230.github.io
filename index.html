About Me

I am a PhD student in the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Science (Yao Class) at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Huanchen Zhang. Before joining Tsinghua, I obtained my BS at Yuanpei College of Peking University, advised by Prof. Tong Yang. My research interests mainly lie in designing lightning-fast data structures and algorithms for storage, databases, and machine learning systems. I recently began designing a high-performance key-value store for HFT systems.

I am actively seeking opportunities in the computer systems research community (academic roles) as well as quantitative research positions in industry. If you find my work relevant, feel free to contact me via email or WeChat.
